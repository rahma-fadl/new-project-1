Qgis

QGIS is a free and open-source geographic information system (GIS) software

used for creating,editing, visualizing, analyzing, and publishing geospatial
It is available for Windows, macOS, Linux, Android, and iOS.

Arc pro

ArcGIS Pro is a professional desktop GIS (geographic information system)

application that provides advanced mapping, analysis, and data management capabilities.
It is developed by Esri and is widely used by professionals in various fields,
including geography, urban planning, environmental science, and natural resource management
